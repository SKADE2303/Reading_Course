about this website

This website was created with the purpose of providing detailed information about solar panel installations at BITS Pilani.

A website that tracks and displays solar panel data is essential for promoting renewable energy awareness and education. By providing comprehensive data on solar panel installations, energy production, and efficiency, this tool helps students, researchers, and the general public understand the impact of solar energy on campus. This information supports academic projects, sustainability initiatives, and can inspire further adoption of renewable energy solutions. With accurate and up-to-date solar panel data, informed decisions can be made to enhance energy management and environmental conservation efforts.
