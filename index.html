about this website

This website was created with the purpose of providing real-time water levels of Ulhas River so residents can be alerted if there is any unusual activity

A website that tracks real-time water levels of a river is crucial for communities, environmental monitoring, and public safety. By providing up-to-date data, such a tool allows residents living near the river to be aware of changing water conditions, which can be essential during periods of heavy rainfall or potential flooding. This information supports emergency services in planning and responding effectively, potentially saving lives and mitigating damage to property. Furthermore, real-time tracking benefits environmental agencies and researchers by supplying continuous data that can aid in the analysis of river health, water management, and ecological conservation. With timely and accurate water level information, informed decisions can be made that protect both human and environmental interests.

Kalyan-Dombivili Metropolitan Area

Maharashtra, India

Flash floods affected several parts of the city including Adharwadi, Katemanivali, and Patripool areas. Emergency evacuation warnings issued for riverside settlements. Water logging reported in multiple localities.

Monsoon Season Flooding

Kalyan-Dombivili Metropolitan Area

Maharashtra, India
